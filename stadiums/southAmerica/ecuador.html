Stadiums in Ecuador

Quick reference of Ecuador

The Republic of Ecuador is a small country in South America. It is a country that is bordered by Colombia and Peru.

Guayaquil is Ecuador largest city and it main port city.

Quito is the capital of Ecuador and it's a city with an elevation of 2,850m (9,350 ft) making it one of the highest city in the world.

Stadiums in Ecuador

The largest stadium in Ecuador is the Estadio Monumental Isidro Romero Carbo. With a capcity of 59,283 located in the city of Guayaquil and opened in the year 1987. Club: Barcelona Sporting Club

Estadio Rodrigo Paz Delgado is the second largest located in Quito with a capacity of 41,575 and opened in the year 1997 Club: Ecuador national football team, and LDU

Estadio Banco del Pacifico Capwell is a stadium that has a capacity of 40,020. Located in Guayaquil and opened in the year of 1945. Club: CS Emelec

Estadio Olimpico Atahualpa located in Quito with a capcaity of 35,742 and opened in the year 1951. Club: América de Quito, SD Quito, El Nacional, Universidad Catôlica del Ecuador
